He lives in Portland, OR.

His work bends traditional notions of composition
and color expression.
It seamlessly incorporates different styles of brushwork
to explore the space between what is real and what is just the feeling.

His work has been described as "bright, but dark".
Asked whether the stories that sometimes accompany his work are true,
he has said "Absolutely they are true. Just not necessarily in the sense of
'this actually was a thing that happened.'"

Lately, he's been thinking about doors.
